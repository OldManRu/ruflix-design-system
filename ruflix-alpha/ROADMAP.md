# RuFlix Alpha Roadmap

> "The best way to experience your personal media."

---

## Vision

RuFlix is not trying to replace Jellyfin.

RuFlix is a premium, TV-first experience built on top of Jellyfin.

Every decision should make browsing your own media feel more cinematic, more intuitive, and more comfortable.

---

# Guiding Principles

- Comfortable over flashy
- Beautiful over clever
- Calm over busy
- Components over pages
- Motion with purpose
- TV-first navigation
- Progressive disclosure
- Every animation earns its place
