# Cinematic Moments

Sprint 2 defines the motion language of RuFlix.

## CM-001 — Application Launch
Purpose: Welcome the user into their personal theater.
- Black screen
- RuFlix logo fades in
- Ambient background begins
- Hero fades into focus
- Navigation appears
- Shelves reveal sequentially
Duration: ~900ms

## CM-002 — Hero Transition
Purpose: Make browsing feel continuous.
- Existing hero gently dims
- Ambient colors morph
- Artwork dissolves
- Title and metadata transition
- Buttons remain anchored
Duration: 350–450ms

## CM-003 — Shelf Focus
Purpose: Reinforce selection.
- Card scales ~4%
- Elevation increases
- Soft reflection appears
- Progress bar brightens
Duration: 120–180ms

## CM-004 — Movie Night
Purpose: Transform browsing into an event.
- User selects Movie Night
- Brief intentional pause
- Optional projector-start sound
- Warm amber glow expands subtly
- Hero atmosphere warms
- "Tonight's Picks" shelf eases into view
- Surprise Me card appears last
- Exit reverses the sequence cleanly

## Motion Rule
Every animation should feel like a camera move, not a software transition.
