# Home Screen

The Home screen is the heart of RuFlix. It should answer one question immediately: "What do I want to watch next?"

## Layout

1. Adaptive left navigation
2. Search at the top
3. Featured hero media
4. Continue Watching shelf
5. Recently Added shelf
6. Family Favorites shelf
7. Additional shelves based on library content

## Hero

- Displays featured artwork with a subtle ambient background derived from the artwork.
- Includes Play, Resume (if applicable), and More Info actions.
- Avoid auto-playing trailers by default.

## Shelves

Shelves should be spacious, horizontally scrollable, and artwork-focused.

Priority:
1. Continue Watching
2. Recently Added
3. Family Favorites
4. Movies
5. TV Shows
6. Live TV (if available)
7. Photos/Music (if available)

## Motion

- Gentle card scaling on focus.
- Smooth shelf scrolling.
- Soft fade transitions.
- Ambient background transitions over 500–1000 ms.
- Motion should be subtle enough that users notice its absence more than its presence.

## Design Rule

Every decision on the Home screen should reduce friction between opening RuFlix and enjoying media.
